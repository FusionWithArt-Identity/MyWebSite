      About Us

      Santiniketan Tribal Academy (STA) was founded by Dr. Koushik Ghosh with a clear vision: uplift Tribal communities. Following the ideals of Gurudev Rabindranath Tagore, we are committed to promoting games, sports, and social work. Our mission goes beyond a single field; we strive for the holistic development of individuals, emphasizing values of education, community service, and self-reliance. Our programs provide a nurturing environment where talent is discovered and cultivated, empowering individuals to become well-rounded, contributing members of society.
